About us

We are committed to providing the best VAS services in Nigeria's telecommunications and information
technology industries.

We are Africa's Technology Power House

Creditswitch is dedicated to providing the best VAS services in Nigeria's telecommunications and information technology industries. Our formidable technical infrastructure and resources enable us to be the country's most preferred VAS service provider and beyond. Due to our adaptable business model and technology, we can serve both B2B and B2C customers from various industries at the same time.

Our solutions and services are appropriate for a wide range of industries, including banking, power service providers, insurance, micro-financing, telecommunications, government, healthcare, service aggregators, lotteries, and others.

Our solutions include airtime and data top-up, SMS, airtime switching, USSD development, shared SMS codes, short code integration, cable TV recharging, and utility bill payments. The services are accessible through multiple service interfaces such as Web APIs, mobile applications, USSD, web portals, SMS, ATM, mobile banking services, and more.

Most of our solutions and services are geared toward financial institutions. As a result, we are committed to providing solutions that prioritize security. Our solutions and services, by default, enable our customers to expand their market service offerings while also providing a competitive advantage, resulting in business growth.
