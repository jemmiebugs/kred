Corporate Social Responsibility

For Creditswitch, corporate social responsibility entails more than simply donating money and resources to the indigent. We believe it begins with us running the business in accordance with sound corporate governance codes and ethics, as embodied in our corporate values and organizational principles. Our CSR activities go a step further by taking into account the environment in which we operate. This is why, for several years, we have been involved in the corporate giving aspect of CSR. As an organization, we have set out to support a variety of social causes.
