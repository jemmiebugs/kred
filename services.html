Our Services

What We Do

SERVICE DETAILS

USSD is universally accessible on any mobile phone. This makes it a powerful tool for data collection and the deployment of software solutions such as financial applications, banking apps, and information systems, among others. We are here to assist you with everything from short code acquisition to development to registration on telecom platforms. We also offer integration with existing shortcodes (shared USSD codes) and USSD APIs.

OUR CORE SERVICES

VIRTUAL TOP-UP

We simplify the sale and distribution of airtime around the world. Whether you are looking to integrate airtime purchases into a web application, provide staff with scheduled airtime to engage clients, or plan to share airtime with family and friends regularly, our solution has been intuitively designed to be the perfect choice for your airtime needs.

SHORTCODE ACQUISITION

Our team of experienced developers will handle the entire shortcode setup process, including the application, approval, Connection and Hosting. Short Code Application Selection and Full "on-call" Support, and Consulting.

USSD SHORTCODE AGGREGATION

We are licensed to use USSD codes to provide Value Added Services (VAS) such as electricity tokens, Educational EPINS, subscription services for cable television and digital entertainment platforms, and betting services, allowing for seamless integration with all telcos through a single connection.

MENU DEVELOPMENT

Our formidable developers will work with your internal team to ensure the smooth development and execution of your USSD MENU in real-time

SHARED & DEDICATED SHORTCODE

we provide dedicated or shared short code service for both B2B AND B2C.
